Wearing The
Academic Hood

Upon receiving your gown and Academic
hood, you'll want to put in on to admire your
distinguished and refined look. Once the gown
is on, the proper wearing of the hood is an
essential part of the overall prestigious look.

  1. Place the hood around your neck and
    shoulders so that the major portion hangs
    down the back and the velvet is up.
  2. For your comfort, you may fasten the cord
    on the front of the hood to your shirt, dress
    or gown to help keep the hood away from
    your neck.
  3. From a frontal view, the velvet border should
    be on top.
  4. To expose the lining, turn the velvet border to
    the outside as shown in blue and white.
